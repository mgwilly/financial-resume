Fun Facts

  1. BYU is the biggest Church School
    • Additionally, BYU is the third biggest private university in the United States
  2. BYU follows an honor code that includes:
    • Be honest
    • Respect others
    • Obey the law and follow campus policy
    • Follow the dress and grooming standards
  3. BYU is located in multiple locations
    • BYU-Provo
    • BYU-Hawaii
    • BYU-Idaho
